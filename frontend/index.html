Privacy & DPDP Compliance Notice

In accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023, this notice explains how we handle your voice recordings and generated reports:

Consent-Based Processing

We process your voice recordings only after obtaining your explicit consent. Checking the consent box and uploading files acts as your clear, affirmative consent.

Purpose Limitation

Your audio is processed strictly to transcribe speech, evaluate pronunciation, and provide diagnostic feedback to help you improve your English pronunciation.

Storage Limitation & Right to Erasure

We do not store your voice data permanently:

  • Audio Recording: The audio file is sent to the backend, processed, and permanently deleted immediately after analysis (usually in less than a minute).
  • Evaluation PDF: The generated PDF report is stored temporarily so you can download it. Reports are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours.
  • Right to Erasure: You can click the "Delete All My Data" button on the results page to instantly delete your report from the server on demand.

Data Security

Audio is processed securely using end-to-end encryption. Your personal voice files are never shared, sold, or used for ads/profiling.
